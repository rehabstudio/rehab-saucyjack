Scroll position detection & triggering module

Tracks the bounding rectangle of an element...

. . . . ￬

...and checking it against a given Y on the viewport...

...so we can do something when either the top, bottom or center of an element...

...intersects an imaginary 'trigger line'...

...such as adding or removing a CSS class and all in a hopefully performant and non-scrolljacky way.

Even trigger your own transitions.
